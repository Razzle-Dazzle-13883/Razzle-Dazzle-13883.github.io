Our Team

When our team was first made, the team had a difficult time coming up with a name. They were stuck between two options so they randomly drew from a list of names the put together. The words razzle dazzle were found and the rest of the name was added later. The team logo was made in 2020 to focus on branding and to emphasize that this would be the first year for the team with second generation members. We went with pink since it was a staple to our team. Our mascot is a unicorn with a lightning horn, to bedazzle the crowd! Meet the dedicated individuals who make up our robotics team. From builders to programmers, each member brings unique skills and passion to our projects.
